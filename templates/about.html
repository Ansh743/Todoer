To add a new task simply follow the below instructions:

  • Enter the title/description of you task in the text feild.
  • Select priority of your task from the dropdown menu.
  • And click the New Task Button

To Mark a task as completed:

  • Click on the tick button at the end of your task title.

To delete a task:

  • Click on the delete button at the end of your task title.
  • Enter the title/description of you task in the text feild.

If you find a bug/issue:

  • Click here to file an issue onto the Todoer GitHub repository.

Thank you for using Todoer!
